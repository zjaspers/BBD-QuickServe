Important Notes:

  • Always check for signs of spoilage (odor, discoloration, texture changes) before using, regardless of the date.
  • Keep all perishable items refrigerated at the recommended temperatures.
  • Use the "First In, First Out" (FIFO) method when stocking ingredients.
  • For items with no expiration date (like salt), check periodically for quality.
  • This checklist should be updated daily as part of standard operating procedures.
